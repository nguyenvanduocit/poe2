# POE2-Leaning Asset Direction

Current assets read as polished European gothic fantasy: library shelves, old books, candles, ivy, tree emblems, ornate gold frames, clean serif typography, and occult academy props. They feel close to classic dark fantasy, but not close enough to the harsher Path of Exile 2 mood.

## Keep

- Dark, readable composition.
- Low-noise center area for stream content.
- Warm firelight as small accents.
- Weathered metal and stone textures.
- Quiet, slow, lofi pacing for the channel identity.

## Reduce

- Gothic cathedral windows.
- Ivy, banners, bookshelves, cozy study props.
- Tree-of-life symbols and academic occult circles.
- Polished gold ornamentation.
- Bright cream serif text that feels like European high fantasy.
- Symmetric decorative corners that feel too elegant.

## Push Toward POE2

- Brutal, ancient, decayed materials: cracked basalt, corroded iron, bone, ash, leather, soot, dried blood, tarnished brass.
- More hostile silhouettes: jagged trim, broken relics, hooked metal, ritual spikes, chipped stone slabs.
- Harsher glyph language: carved marks, primitive runes, scratched sigils, blood-painted symbols, scorched inscriptions.
- Less “wizard study”, more “forsaken shrine / ruined encampment / cursed reliquary”.
- Desaturated palette: charcoal, blackened green, oxidized teal, ember orange, old bone, dried rust.
- Asymmetry and damage: broken corners, missing inlays, uneven scratches, ash deposits.

## Path of Lofi Balance

The scene should still be usable for a calm tech stream. Do not make it too busy, horror-heavy, or gore-forward. The right target is:

> POE2-inspired dark relic UI, but slowed down and cleaned up enough for a lofi stream overlay.

## Prompt Pattern

Use this pattern for new image generation:

```text
Dark fantasy livestream overlay for "Path of Lofi", inspired by Path of Exile 2 mood, not European gothic academy. Ancient ruined shrine, cracked basalt wall, corroded iron frame, tarnished brass details, bone and ash accents, primitive carved runes, scorched glyphs, ember firelight, oxidized teal highlights, worn leather, chipped stone, asymmetrical damage, brutal relic UI, quiet lofi atmosphere, large clean empty center area for video content, readable layout, cinematic low-key lighting, high detail, 16:9.

Avoid: cathedral windows, ivy, cozy library, bookshelves, tree-of-life emblem, polished gold ornaments, elegant European filigree, clean occult academy symbols, high fantasy royal style, bright magical glow, cluttered center.
```

## Scene-Specific Notes

- `starting-soon`: ruined stone desk or altar, one ember brazier, cracked inscription slab, title left or center-left.
- `be-right-back`: darker and simpler, fewer props, one damaged sign plate, strong empty space.
- `offline`: schedule panel can look like a carved stone tablet or corroded metal notice board, not a polished framed parchment.
- `live-overlay-frame`: keep transparent center, replace elegant corners with broken iron/stone brackets and small oxidized teal glyph accents.
- `facebook-story-going-live`: stronger vertical monument composition, but avoid cathedral/castle silhouettes; use shrine wall, relic plaque, ash and ember.

# Facebreaker

Patch 0.5 **Return of the Ancients** (launch 2026-05-29) remakes Facebreaker from POE1 into POE2 unique gloves[^1] — no longer a +%damage stick but a **build-enabler item**. The core mod: **while equipped with empty hands, you can use every mace skill with your fists**[^1]. It comes with a **boss teeth tracker** on the map — each boss you've pulverised grants the character added physical damage, creating progression-based scaling that runs through the whole campaign. Strength stacking applies to both an offensive and a defensive layer. Two build targets are clear straight from the trailer: a **Monk Martial Artist** using the :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Way_of_the_Stonefist"} Way of the Stonefist ascendancy notable to make up its thin stat pool, and a **Warrior/Titan** strength-stacker using :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Hammer_of_the_Gods"} Hammer of the Gods but with a punch animation (the trailer joke "Fist of the Gods"[^1]).

## Why This Item Is Powerful

POE1 Facebreaker was a **flat damage multiplier** (+600-800% unarmed damage, pushing unarmed up to weapon-level endgame). POE2 Facebreaker changes direction — it doesn't give damage directly but opens up **qualitative access** + a **progression-scaling stack**. The 3 power layers:

**Layer 1 — Mace skill access without weapon constraint.** POE2's weapon-class gate is fairly strict: mace skills like :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Hammer_of_the_Gods"} Hammer of the Gods, :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Sunder"} Sunder, :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Boneshatter"} Boneshatter, :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Earthshatter"} Earthshatter, :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Leap_Slam"} Leap Slam, and :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Tectonic_Slams"} Tectonic Slams all require a mace equipped. Facebreaker **removes** this requirement — empty hands can use them too. The consequence: damage skills scale with character stats (strength + added phys) rather than weapon DPS gating. The build can invest everything into the gloves slot, chest slot, and jewels — no need to chase a mirror-tier mace.

**Layer 2 — Progression-scaling boss teeth.** A mechanic that never existed in POE1/POE2 before. Bosses you've killed are tracked on the map; each boss grants added physical damage. The math chain is a rough estimate until the concrete values leak: if the POE2 campaign has ~25 bosses (10 act-end bosses + 15 mini-bosses) and each grants 5-15 added phys, the total endgame stack = 125-375 flat added phys. Combined with strength scaling and mace skill base damage = exponential scaling as the player reaches endgame. Especially strong for a character starting a fresh league — the leveling experience directly boosts endgame DPS.

**Layer 3 — Strength offensive + defensive double-duty.** :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Strength"} Strength in POE2 has a base bonus of +2 max life / 1 strength + 0.2% melee damage / 1 strength. Facebreaker adds a further modifier that scales with strength in both directions — meaning each strength point gains 3 times over: the base strength bonus + Facebreaker offensive scaling + Facebreaker defensive scaling. A :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Titan"} Titan ascendancy build already has a notable pushing strength → +1% AOE/effect → now add Facebreaker × strength = a quadruple-dip.

## Build Enabler Mechanics

3 main archetypes:

**A. Monk Martial Artist Stonefist + Facebreaker** — directly matches 2 just-revealed mechanics.

:wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Way_of_the_Stonefist"} Way of the Stonefist (Martial Artist notable) transforms equipped gloves prefix/suffix into "more powerful versions of related modifiers"[^2]. Stonefist scales gloves stats up — Facebreaker base + Stonefist multiplier = the strongest gloves slot in the game at endgame. The Monk already channels skills with bare hands, and now adds mace skill access = the full Warrior skill pool available on a Monk character. A Hammer of the Gods Monk = the combo of Way of the Stonefist (gloves) + Facebreaker (gloves) + Martial Artist mirage skill stacking — this could be the day-1 OP build.

**B. Warrior/Titan strength-stack with Hammer of the Gods**.

The Titan ascendancy is a strength-stacking core, and Facebreaker fills the gloves slot granting double-duty offense/defense. Build path: invest in strength through the passive tree (Strength of the Bear, Strength of Stone nodes), Voidstones for a strength implicit, jewels with +strength. The main skill is Hammer of the Gods (the visual "Fist of the Gods" in the trailer[^1]) — slam damage scales with strength × added phys (boss teeth). Defensive layer = high life + armour. Tank skills with bare hands instead of a 2H mace.

**C. Hollow Palm/Unarmed Monk legacy build path**.

In POE1, Hollow Palm Technique was a keystone passive for unarmed melee. POE2 0.4 already had a Hollow Palm Monk emerging[^3]. Facebreaker reopens this path but shifts the focus to **mace skill access** rather than pure unarmed strikes. The build uses mace skills (Sunder/Boneshatter) with bare hands + Monk movement skills (Whirling Blow, Tempest Bell) — a hybrid unarmed-mace. It's unclear whether Hollow Palm ports over to POE2 — something to confirm in the patch notes.

Builds that don't suit Facebreaker: **any character that needs a weapon-specific stat** (bow Ranger, staff Witch, dual-wield duelist) because the empty-hands clause excludes weapons. **Crit-focused mace builds** — POE2 crit hasn't yet been rebalanced for unarmed, so weapon-based crit may still beat flat added-phys scaling.

## Acquisition

The drop source hasn't been revealed by GGG. POE1 Facebreaker dropped from the general unique pool — the POE2 version is likely also a global pool. League launch patterns:

1. **Drop from a campaign boss** — if boss teeth is a core mechanic, Facebreaker may be locked behind 1 specific boss (the Brutus-of-Act-1 logic) → explaining the "Red Wolf" and "Ezomytes vs Eternal Empire" lore
2. **Verisium Anvil forge** — the 0.5 league mechanic allows forging uniques[^4], and Facebreaker could be in the forge result pool
3. **Random global** — an extremely low drop rate, the common unique pool

The week-1 launch price is hard to predict pre-launch. The 0.4 first-wave build-enabling unique pattern usually settles at 2-10 divine after a week. If Monk Stonefist + Facebreaker becomes a day-1 meta build: the price spikes to 10-20d in the first week, settling at ~3-5d after 2 weeks. If the meta doesn't pick it up: ~1-2d.

## Version History

### Patch 0.5.0 (Return of the Ancients — 2026-05-29)

Item introduced as the POE2 remake of POE1 Facebreaker. Mod values, base type, level requirement, and the exact strength scaling formula are not yet public. The core mechanic has shifted from a flat unarmed damage multiplier to **mace skill access + a progression boss teeth tracker + strength double-duty**.

### POE1 Facebreaker (reference)

The POE1 version was a Strapped Mitts unique granting a large % increased physical damage with unarmed attacks + an accuracy bonus + unarmed-attack ailment damage scaling[^6]. Build core: empty-hands + slam/strike skills compatible with the unarmed attack tag. The POE2 version diverges sharply — it doesn't give a damage multiplier directly, focusing instead on mace skill access (qualitative) and progression scaling (boss teeth) rather than a flat damage buff.

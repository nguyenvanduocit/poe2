Character snapshots per league/patch for POE1 and POE2. PoB-calculated numbers (pob1/pob2) are fetched from the GGG API or poe.ninja snapshots to get real figures; bundled with gear, pob code, and a hand-written journal. Old characters (POE1 Mirage, Keepers...) are kept to reference build choices across leagues. POE2 0.5+ will get a new entry once a 0.5 character is created and its pob exported.

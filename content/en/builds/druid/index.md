The POE2 Druid revolves around shapeshift forms, totems, and minions via Sacred Flow. It differs from the Witch in that Druid builds are hybrid — pets plus yourself participate in the damage through shapeshifting — rather than commanding pets purely like the POE1 Necromancer.

## Ascendancy

- **Shaman** — totem-spirit focus, scaling minions via Sacred Flow. The Druid's main pet/swarm build.
- **Warden** — defensive/support shapeshifting, animal form as a survivability layer.
- **Oracle** — elemental + buff aura.

POE2 Sorceress is the classic Int caster — spell, elemental damage, with a few ascendancies pivoting into minions. It's the most versatile class in POE2 because its gem pool supports elemental, chaos, and minions alike through spirit gems.

## Ascendancy

- **Disciple of Varashta** — minion-focused, immortal Djinn pets, chaos→fire conversion.
- **Stormweaver** — lightning + elemental specialist, ailment scaling.
- **Chronomancer** — time/cooldown manipulation, buff auras, support-heavy.

POE2 Warrior is Str melee — armour, block, warcry, slow-and-heavy hits. It differs from POE1's Marauder in damage philosophy: POE2 melee is designed around hitting-precisely over hitting-fast, each swing costs meaningful stamina/mana, and mis-timing means death.

## Ascendancy

- **Warbringer** — warcry-focused, scales damage through warcry exertion. A strong leveling/campaign pick.
- **Titan** — defensive/offensive endgame scaling (needs a respec from Warbringer).
- **Smith** — crafting/gear-oriented (needs a respec).

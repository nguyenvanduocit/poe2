# Hollow Mask Acolyte Minion

:wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/The_Hollow_Mask"} turns :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Acolyte_of_Chayula"} into one of the most unique minion scalers in POE2. The build uses :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Into_the_Breach"} to continuously spawn purple :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Flames_of_Chayula"}, and then the helmet itself shares the entire remnant effect to every minion in your Presence. The result is an army that gains 80-150%+ damage as extra Chaos while you keep an extremely strong chaos defense layer from :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Chayula's_Gift"} and near-automatic flask sustain from :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Wildwood's_Gifts"}.

This is a true **Eldritch Horror Summoner** archetype: hybrid remnant + spectre/minion chaos, volatility triggers combined with a player staff skill, all revolving around stably sharing Purple Flames after GGG fixed remnant sharing.

## Build Overview

The build's damage loop comes down to four clear steps.

First, go Monk, pick :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Acolyte_of_Chayula"} and allocate :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Waking_Dream"} — this node grants :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Into_the_Breach"}, a persistent reservation skill. When active, the skill creates a Breach around you and spawns one :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Flame_of_Chayula"} every 1.21-1.4 seconds (the rate improves with gem level). The Flame carries the Remnant tag and fades on its own after 25 seconds if not picked up.

Next is :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Lucid_Dreaming"} + :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Choice_of_Power"}. These two nodes lock every Flame to Purple, increase Remnant effect by 50%, and extend the pickup range by 50%. Each Purple Flame gives the character (and now minions) a stack of 7% damage as extra Chaos, lasting 8 seconds, stacking up to 10 times — a total of 70% base before any multipliers.

The crux belongs to :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/The_Hollow_Mask"}. The mod line "Remnants you create affect Allies in your Presence as well as you when collected" makes Flames of Chayula share the entire Purple buff with every minion (and ally) standing within Presence radius. Combine :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Empowering_Remnants"} (15% chance to grant effect twice) and link :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Remnant_Potency_I"} into Into the Breach, and the total extra Chaos on the army easily exceeds 100-150% depending on investment.

Defense revolves around :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Chayula's_Gift"} — a node that grants +10% maximum Chaos Resistance and also doubles your chaos res stat, pushing the cap easily to 85%+ on just average gear. :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/The_Hollow_Mask"} drags -10% all elemental resistances, so you have to overcap ele res, but in exchange the build is nearly immune to chaos damage (its own primary damage type). The extra 15% additional Physical Damage Reduction from the helmet is a free tankiness layer.

Wildwood's Gifts (the level 19 skill from the helmet) continuously spawns Ancient Blooms on its own, providing steady flask charges — nearly eliminating flask sustain issues during long mapping.

## Skill Gems & Links

**Core skill from the ascendancy:**

- :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Into_the_Breach"} (Waking Dream) — link :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Remnant_Potency_I"} + :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Harmonic_Remnants"} to increase effect and pickup range. The 94% increased Reservation Efficiency of Remnant Skills on the helmet makes this skill's spirit cost nearly negligible.

**Minion pool (priority: keep within Presence 8-12m):**

- Raise Spectre with a fitting chaos spectre (Eldritch theme) — scales spell + minion damage, receives the full Purple Flame buff. This is the popular direction of the "Eldritch Horror Summoner" archetype.

- :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Skeletal_Storm_Mage"} + :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Shock_Conduction"} + :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Overcharge"} — shock bot, lightning damage that then adds extra Chaos very naturally.

- :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Raging_Spirits"} cluster — large numbers, each one takes the full extra Chaos, extremely strong map clear despite low individual damage.

**Hybrid variant (currently popular):**

Combine a staff skill for the player (volatility trigger or a chaos spell like the Incinerate variant) with minions for clear and single target. Many builds use Cast on Minion Death + Wolf Pack or Raise Spectre alongside.

**Aura & Utility:**

- :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Grim_Feast"} for minion revive sustain (Remnant-based, synergizes with Hollow Mask sharing) or a Herald depending on the minion pool's main damage type.

- :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Ghost_Dance"} + :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Upwelling"} for ES recovery on dodge, matching Acolyte's hybrid evasion/ES profile.

- :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Despair"} (manual cast or via the Cursed Ground support) for bosses to shred enemy chaos res.

- :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Mana_Remnants"} reservation is nearly free thanks to the helmet mod, opening room for a second minion gem or a strong aura.

## Ascendancy

The four-point lab order:

**1. Waking Dream** — Absolutely mandatory. Without this node there's no Into the Breach, no Flames of Chayula, nothing left to discuss.

**2. Lucid Dreaming + Choice of Power** — Take immediately after the first point. Choice of Power locks every Flame to Purple (stable damage type), increases remnant effect by 50%, and extends pickup range by 50%. This is the biggest multiplier for all the extra Chaos on the army.

**3. Chayula's Gift** — An indispensable defense layer. +10% max chaos res and doubled chaos res make capping at 85%+ very easy. Skipping this node is suicide because chaos damage double-dips into ES and The Hollow Mask already gives -10% elemental res.

**4. Final point** — :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Sap_of_Nightmares"} (leech applies to chaos damage too) is the safest choice. :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Inner_Turmoil"} is only worth it if the build inflicts elemental ailments frequently. :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Embrace_the_Darkness"} devours the entire Spirit pool and directly conflicts with Into the Breach, so it's almost never used in the minion variant.

## Passive Tree & Mastery

The path from the Monk starting area (heading East-Southeast) crosses up into the Witch region (Northwest) to reach the minion damage, minion life, and spirit reservation efficiency clusters.

The key stop along the way is the notable **Empowering Remnants** near the Mind & Body cluster — 15% chance for Remnants to grant effect twice, averaging an extra ~15% multiplier on the Purple Flame buff. The path isn't too far off-route and delivers clear value.

The price you pay is being 15-20 passive points further out compared to Witch Infernalist (whose minion clusters sit right next to the starting area). In exchange you get unique extra Chaos scaling that Infernalist doesn't have, plus top-tier chaos defense by current standards.

## Stat Priorities & Defenses

- **ES / Life:** Hybrid evasion + ES, targeting 5,000-8,000+ ES with lvl 80+ gear.

- **Armour / Evasion:** Evasion mainly from base Monk + body armor + boots. The Hollow Mask only contributes a little (18 Evasion + 12 ES).

- **Block / Spell Block:** Not native, skip.

- **Chaos Resistance:** 85%+ cap (easy to reach thanks to Chayula's Gift doubled res).

- **Elemental Resistances:** Overcap 90%+ (offsetting the -10% from the helmet).

- **Physical Damage Reduction:** 15% additional from The Hollow Mask — a free layer well worth having.

- **EHP:** High vs chaos damage; moderate vs elemental. Ghost Dance + evasion keep recovery solid.

- **Spirit:** Very important and tight. Into the Breach + minion gems + auras compete fiercely — the 94% Remnant Reservation Efficiency on the helmet is a lifeline.

- **Movement Speed:** Baseline Monk + good boots, no special boost from the ascendancy.

## Gear Progression

### Leveling

Hollow Palm Quarterstaff up to Act 3 (once you have enough 2 ascendancy points to open Waking Dream + Lucid Dreaming). After that you can keep an Acolyte chaos caster fallback (Volatility stacking) while farming or trading for The Hollow Mask.

### Early Mapping

Without The Hollow Mask the build doesn't run yet. Use a chaos caster Acolyte variant or another Monk as a placeholder until you have the helmet. This is the build's biggest weakness.

### Endgame

- **Helm (mandatory):** :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/The_Hollow_Mask"} — corrupt prioritizing extra spirit or increased remnant effect / reservation efficiency.

- **Body Armor:** :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Reverie"} (good for player survivability despite recovery being self-only) or rare life + ES + chaos res + spirit.

- **Weapon:** Sceptre with local minion damage + spirit on kill, or a hybrid staff for the player skill (Raven staff style volatility/chaos trigger).

- **Jewels & rares:** T1 minion damage, minion life, spirit reservation efficiency, chaos res, ele res overcap, evasion rating, life/ES.

### Mirror Tier

Double-corrupt The Hollow Mask (extra spirit + remnant potency), mirror rares full T1 minion + spirit, +2 minion gems amulet or helmet corruption if available.

## Flasks

Life flask for emergencies, mana flask for channeled aura/reservation, charm slot for stun/freeze immunity. :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Wildwood's_Gifts"} (level 19 from the helmet) spawns Ancient Blooms continuously — flask charges almost never run out during long mapping.

## Pantheon & Bandits

POE2 doesn't have a Pantheon system. Bandit rewards in the campaign usually take passive chaos resistance or spirit — pick according to your spirit budget at the time.

## Leveling Notes

- Act 1-2: Hollow Palm Quarterstaff + early ascendancy point into Waking Dream or Inner Turmoil.

- Act 3: You have 2 points — start watching The Hollow Mask price. If the price drops fast, consider swapping to the minion path early.

- Act 4+: Full minion swap if you have the helmet and enough spirit. If not, keep the caster fallback until you can farm the helm.

## Budget & Investment

The Hollow Mask is the build's only gate.

- Launch +0 to +5: high hype price (20-50 div) — wait for community testing.

- After the interaction is confirmed stable: price drops to 10-20 div — a reasonable entry point for medium budget.

- After that it settles at utility-tier value (Wildwood flask sustain + 94% Remnant Reservation Efficiency) — 5-15 div depending on corrupt.

Budget scaling is average because, once you have the helmet, gear is mostly rare minion + spirit, with no other expensive uniques needed beyond the helm.

## Strengths & Limitations

**Strengths:**

- Scaling extra Chaos 80-150%+ across the entire minion army is a nearly unique mechanic in POE2 right now, extremely strong when combined with chaos res shred.

- Flask sustain is nearly passive thanks to Ancient Bloom from Wildwood's Gifts.

- Chaos tankiness is top tier (85%+ cap easily + 15% additional phys DR + Ghost Dance recovery).

- Unique playstyle, combining remnant + summon + hybrid player-minion.

**Limitations:**

- You have to travel 15-20 points to get minion scaling (large opportunity cost compared to Infernalist Witch).

- The spirit budget is extremely tight — many gems have to be cut or sacrificed.

- Poor mobility, no movement skill or dash from the ascendancy.

- Weak league start — needs a late unique + lots of lab points + far pathing.

- Infernalist Witch and Spirit Walker Huntress are still stronger at pure minion DPS or easier companion scaling.

## Summary

- The build uses :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/The_Hollow_Mask"} to share Purple Flames of Chayula with the minion army, creating massive extra Chaos of 80-150%+.

- :wiki-link{url="https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Chayula's_Gift"} + Wildwood's Gifts are two extremely strong defense and utility layers.

- Suits players who like the Eldritch Horror minion chaos archetype and accept far pathing and tight spirit management.

- A medium-budget all-rounder once you have the helmet; not a safe league starter.

- After GGG fixed remnant sharing, the build went from hypothesis to a real niche pick on the ladder.

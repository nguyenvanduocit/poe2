# Donate

## Why this site exists

This site started as my own **personal second brain for PoE gameplay** — where I note down builds, farming strategies, mechanics, character progress, and everything I teach myself each league. After a few leagues that body of knowledge got large enough that I figured: instead of hiding it in a local Obsidian vault, I might as well make it public for anyone else who plays PoE.

The core purpose isn't traffic, isn't money — it's **learning for myself by writing things down**. That's why the writing here deliberately follows a *tutorial-that-explains-why* style, not a reference dump — every mechanic comes with its reasoning, every build choice with its argument, every farming strategy with the economics behind it. You can see that most clearly on pages like [Builds](/builds), [Farming](/farming), and [Mechanics](/mechanics).

The whole pipeline is also augmented with AI skills (PoB calc, trade lookup, build import from poe.ninja/mobalytics, wiki search) so I can take notes faster — but every gameplay decision is still mine; the AI only fetches real data and crunches DPS/EHP. If you're curious about the character I'm playing this league, see [TheLeader_A](/characters/the-leader-a) — a Witch Necromancer CI Spectre, an endgame build pushing 40/40 challenges.

## What a donation means to me

I make **no** money from this site: no ads, no affiliate links, no trackers. It runs as a public knowledge base, free for anyone to read, no one has to pay anything.

So a donation has exactly one meaning: you found it valuable and want to toss me a bit of divine so I keep investing the time to write — not an obligation. **Not donating is completely fine too**, read freely, share freely, copy builds freely.

If you still want to donate, here are the 2 in-game ways.

## Option 1 — Donate a Divine Orb (simplest)

Since donations sit outside real trade, I don't list items on the trade site — you whisper my active character directly. This league (**Mirage 3.28**), my in-game name is `TheLeader_A`.

The process:

1. **Whisper in game**: type `@TheLeader_A donate` (or any message) in chat. I'll see the whisper and send a party invite.
2. **Join the party and wait for me to reach the hideout** — or come to mine, either direction works.
3. **Open the trade window** (`Ctrl+click` my character in the party panel → Trade With), drop the Divine Orb in your slot, leave mine empty.
4. **Both hit Accept**. Trade complete.

Note: if I'm offline, the whisper won't send — try Vietnam evening hours (after 8pm) when I'm online most. If I'm AFK in the hideout, ping again after a few minutes.

## Option 2 — Donate an Item

Exactly the same as Option 1, only step 3 differs: instead of a Divine Orb, you drop any item into the trade slot. I'm not picky — every kind of item is fine, including:

- **Other currency**: Chaos Orb, Exalted Orb, Mirror Shard, scarab, fragment, Awakened gem
- **High maps**: T16, T17, pre-juiced maps
- **Uniques I'm using**: if you check my [character page](/characters/the-leader-a) and see an upgrade I'm hunting (e.g. a Watcher's Eye with better mods), I'd be very grateful
- **Mageblood, Headhunter, mirror-tier items**: if you have a spare — kidding, no expectations, but I won't refuse either

Donated items go straight into playing the league further, or get sold to fund research into a new build I'll write about later (closed loop: you donate → I test → I write → you read).

## Account vs Character — don't mix them up

My account name is `hopthuxacnhan#3062`, used for the **friend list** (Social panel → Add Friend). The character name `TheLeader_A` is what you use to **whisper in game**. These are two different things:

- Add the friend account (`hopthuxacnhan#3062`) → shows in the friend panel, can message me through the game even when I'm playing a different character

For a quick donation, **whispering the character** is the fewest steps. If I switch characters next league (very likely — I re-roll every league), check the [character page](/characters/the-leader-a) for the latest IGN; the account stays fixed forever.

## I don't accept out-of-game donations

No Patreon, no Ko-fi, no PayPal, no bank transfer. The reason is simple: this site is about PoE, and donating inside PoE is **the most natural** for both sides — you use the currency I'm playing with to support the very thing I'm playing. No divine ↔ USD ↔ VND exchange rates to convert, no tax to worry about, nothing to overthink.

If you want to support in another way (feedback, fixing errors, suggesting topics you'd like me to write about), just whisper me in game — feedback is worth as much as divine, if not more.

# Spectre Shaman — Druid Shaman

---

## Build Overview

### Identity

- **Primary Skill:** Raise Spectre (Vulgard → Powered Zealots)
- **Damage Type:** Elemental (Grenade/Fire/Lightning)
- **Playstyle:** Minion Summoner (Passive clear style)
- **Budget Tier:** League Starter (0 currency required)
- **League:** Vaal | **Patch:** 0.4 | **Updated:** 2025-12-16

### Character Profile

**Character:** GhazzyTV_Awooo
**Profile Link:** https://poe.ninja/poe2/builds/vaal/character/Ghazzy-0404/GhazzyTV_Awooo

### Description

Build dùng Sacred Flow của Druid Shaman để đạt 356-456 Spirit, đủ summon một dàn Specter tự clear phần lớn nội dung. Nhịp chơi là caster ở Act 1-2, chuyển sang Specter từ Act 3 (Utzaal), dùng Vulgard Specters để leveling/maps rồi đổi sang Powered Zealots ở endgame khi attack speed đã đủ cao.

**Why This Build:**
- Leveling từ Act 3 trở đi rất nhẹ
- Không cần gear bắt buộc, chạy được với 0 currency
- Gameplay đơn giản: summon minion, để chúng clear
- Dễ vào cho người mới vì ít yêu cầu thao tác
- Có đầu tư thì scale tiếp, không đầu tư vẫn qua campaign/maps sớm

**Who This Is For:**
- Người cần league starter ổn định
- Người mới chơi PoE2 muốn build ít phạt lỗi
- Người thích minion và passive clearing
- Người muốn leveling dễ chạy hơn là cơ chế phức tạp

### Pros & Cons

**Strengths**
- **League starter mạnh** - Không cần currency hoặc unique bắt buộc
- **Leveling dễ** - Từ Act 3 trở đi boss và pack chết nhanh
- **Thân thiện với người mới** - Gameplay đơn giản, minion tự tank và clear
- **Scale tốt** - Sacred Flow cho 356-456 Spirit để tăng số lượng minion
- **Ổn định** - Không dựa quá nhiều vào RNG
- **Ít phạt lỗi** - Minion body-block và tank giúp người chơi có thêm khoảng thở

**Weaknesses**
- **Acts 1-2 struggle** - Must play caster until Act 3 Specters
- **Moderate mobility** - Not a zoom-zoom build, minions need to catch up
- **Spirit management** - Must not equip Charms to maximize Spirit (Sacred Flow)
- **Vendor checking** - Need to frequently check vendors for +Minion gear

## Skills & Tree

### Ascendancy

**Shaman**

| Trial | Level | Node | Priority | Benefit |
|-------|-------|------|----------|---------|
| First Trial (Act 2) | 20-30 | **Sacred Flow** | **MUST HAVE** | Provides 40+ Spirit per empty Charm slot. Core mechanic enabling massive Spirit pool (356-456 total). **DO NOT EQUIP CHARMS!** |
| Second Trial | 50+ | TBD | High | Additional Shaman nodes for minion buffs or survivability |

**Ascendancy Notes:**
- **Sacred Flow is the entire build** - This single node provides 120 Spirit (40 x 3 charm slots)
- Get a belt with 2 Charm slots + Valley of the Titans (Act 2) quest reward (+1 Charm) = 3 total slots
- **CRITICAL:** Leave all Charm slots EMPTY to maximize Spirit
- With 356-456 Spirit, you can summon 6-8 Specters (55 Spirit each) plus support minions

### Gem Setups

#### Leveling Setup (Acts 1-2): Caster

**Before Specters:**
```
Entangle (Primary crowd control)
├─ Basic supports as available

Thunderstorm (After The Executioner boss, Manor Ramparts)
├─ Basic supports as available

Orb of Storms (Secondary damage)
├─ Basic supports

Optional: Skeletal Sniper Minions (Act 2+)
├─ For passive damage
```

**Playstyle:** Alternate Entangle and Thunderstorm to Overgrow enemies. Keep vines active (short duration after Wildsurge Incantation keystone).

#### Main Setup (Acts 3+): Vulgard Specters

**6-Link (Primary Damage) - Vulgard Phase:**
```
Raise Spectre: Vulgard (50 Spirit, Grenade/Wing variant)
├─ Multi-shot - EXTREMELY IMPORTANT (adds projectiles)
├─ Muster Support - Increases minion count
├─ Feeding Frenzy - Minion aggression and damage
├─ Heft Support - Increases minion power
└─ Magnified Area - Huge AoE boost with NO damage reduction
```

**Support Setup:**
```
Pain Offering
└─ Auto-targets Skeleton Warriors for damage buff (no supports needed)

Vulnerability (Curse)
└─ Cast on tough targets/bosses for armor break
```

**Where to Get Vulgard:**
- **Location:** Utzaal zone (Late Act 3)
- **Must be EXACTLY 50 Spirit cost** - Grenade or Wing variant
- Use Desecrate/Unearth to spawn corpses, then Raise Spectre

#### Endgame Setup: Powered Zealots

**6-Link (Primary Damage) - Powered Zealots:**
```
Raise Spectre: Powered Zealots (55 Spirit, Lightning Beam variant)
├─ Multi-shot - CRITICAL (lightning beams projectiles)
├─ Muster Support - More minions
├─ Feeding Frenzy - Aggression + damage
├─ Rapid Attacks - Attack speed (no cooldown like Vulgard)
└─ Fire Enchantment - Elemental damage boost
```

**When to Transition:**
- Mid-maps when you have high attack speed
- **Why:** Vulgard grenades have 2-second cooldown - wasted with high attack speed
- **Why Powered Zealots:** No cooldown on lightning beams, scales with attack speed

**Where to Get Powered Zealots:**
- **Location:** Etched Ravine (Third Interlude)
- **Must be 55 Spirit cost**
- Complete Third Interlude first for 40 bonus Spirit from Lythara the Wayward Spear (Kriar Village)

### Passive Tree Strategy

**Priority Order (After Act 3 respec to minions):**

**Phase 1: Defense Foundation**
1. Energy Shield nodes (tấm chắn năng lượng)
2. Armor nodes (giáp)
3. Life nodes

**Phase 2: Basic Minion Power**
1. Crystine Flesh
2. Restless Dead
3. Growing Swarm

**Phase 3: Minion Scaling**
1. Gravedigger
2. Necrotized Flesh
3. Grant Has Fallen

**Phase 4: Elite Nodes**
1. **Lord of Horrors** (Rush this)
2. Vile Bending (minion durability)
3. Pick Incarnation (secondary damage)

**Phase 5: Spectre Optimization**
- Minion damage nodes
- Stun buildup nodes
- Armor break nodes
- Area of effect nodes

**Respec Warning:** Save gold for Act 3 respec when transitioning from caster to Specters. Significant point reallocation required.

## Gear Roadmap

### General Gear Priorities (All Phases)

**CHECK VENDORS CONSTANTLY** - This is the most important tip.

**Stat Priority:**
1. **Spirit** - Most important stat, enables more minions
2. **+Level to Minion Skills** - Massive damage increase
3. **Life** - Survivability
4. **Resistances** - Cap at 75%
5. **Energy Shield / Armor** - Defense layers

### Phase 1: Acts 1-2 (Caster)

**Weapon:**
- Wands or Staves
- Look for: +Level to All Spell Skills OR +Level to Physical Spell Skills
- % Increased Spell Damage

**Target Stats:** Enough damage to clear acts comfortably

### Phase 2: Act 3+ (Transition to Specters)

**Weapon:**
- **Scepter** (preferred) + Shield combo, OR
- Wand/Scepter dual wield
- Look for: +Level to All Minion Skills, % Increased Spirit

**Helm:**
- **+Level to All Minion Skills** (priority #1)

**Amulet:**
- **+Level to All Minion Skills** (priority #1)

**Body Armor:**
- **+Spirit** (priority #1)
- Life, resistances

**Belt:**
- **2 Charm Slots** (for Sacred Flow)
- Life, resistances

**Boots:**
- **% Increased Movement Speed**
- Life, resistances

**Rings/Other:**
- Life, resistances, Spirit if possible

**Target Stats:** 3500+ life, 75% resistances, 200-300+ Spirit from gear

### Phase 3: Maps (Budget Optimization)

**Key Upgrades:**
1. Better Scepter with higher +Minion levels and Spirit
2. Helm with +2 Minion Skills
3. Amulet with +2 Minions and Spirit
4. Body Armor with high Spirit roll
5. Consider Corrupted items with +Spirit or +1 Minion

**Target Stats:** 4500+ life, 75% resistances, 300-400 Spirit from gear

### Phase 4: Endgame (High Investment)

**Min-Max Goals:**
1. **Helm:** +2 Minion Skills, Corrupted for +Spirit or +1 Minion
2. **Amulet:** Solar Amulet with +2 Minions, Spirit, Anointed (Mana recovery on kill)
3. **Weapon:** Rattling Scepter (Unique) - Provides 2 free Skeleton Warriors
4. **Body Armor:** Conjurer's Mantle (Unique) - Spirit + reduced reservation
5. **Rings:** Focus on Spirit, Life, Resistances
6. **Jewels:** Mana Recovery per Kill (for Mind Over Matter synergy)

**Target Stats:** 5000+ life, 75% all res, 400-500 Spirit from gear

## Leveling Path

### Act-by-Act Strategy

#### Acts 1-2: Caster Foundation

**Main Skills:**
- **Entangle** - Primary crowd control and damage
- **Thunderstorm** - Unlocked after The Executioner (Manor Ramparts)
- **Orb of Storms** - Secondary damage source
- **Unearth** - Spawning corpses (prepare for Specters)

**Playstyle:**
- Alternate Entangle → Thunderstorm to apply Overgrow
- Keep vines active (short duration post-Wildsurge Incantation)
- Add Skeletal Sniper minions in Act 2 for passive DPS

**Key Milestone:**
- Complete **Trial of the Sekhemas** (Act 2, level 20-30)
- Choose **Shaman** ascendancy
- Take **Sacred Flow** as first node
- Get belt with 2 Charm slots
- Complete **Valley of the Titans** quest for +1 Charm slot reward
- **Total: 3 Charm slots × 40 Spirit = 120 Spirit from Sacred Flow**

**Gear Focus:**
- Wands/Staves with +Spell levels and % Spell Damage
- Basic life and resistances

#### Act 3: The Golden Transition

**Critical Moment: Utzaal Zone (Late Act 3)**

**Step-by-Step Transition:**

1. **Reach Utzaal zone**
2. **Find Vulgard Specters:**
   - Use Desecrate/Unearth to spawn corpses
   - Look for Vulgard with **EXACTLY 50 Spirit cost**
   - Variants: Grenade thrower or Wing variant
   - Raise 2-3 Vulgard initially
3. **Respec passive tree:**
   - Save gold beforehand for respec cost
   - Remove caster nodes
   - Invest in minion damage, life, ES, armor
   - Priority: Crystine Flesh, Restless Dead, Growing Swarm
4. **Update gem setup:**
   - 6-link Vulgard with Multi-shot, Muster, Feeding Frenzy, Heft, Magnified Area
   - Add Pain Offering, Vulnerability
5. **Swap gear:**
   - Replace Wands/Staves with Scepter/Shield
   - Find +Minion levels on Helm/Amulet from vendors
   - Prioritize Spirit on body armor

**Expected Result:**
- Từ Act 3 trở đi leveling nhẹ hơn hẳn
- Vulgard Specters xử lý boss campaign rất nhanh
- Passive playstyle: minion làm phần lớn công việc

**Gem Setup (Complete):**
```
6L: Raise Spectre (Vulgard) + Multi-shot + Muster + Feeding Frenzy + Heft + Magnified Area
Support: Pain Offering
Curse: Vulnerability
```

#### Acts 4-10: Cruise Control

**Main Strategy:**
- Maintain Vulgard Specters (recapture if you die/lose them)
- Upgrade gear from vendors (check constantly!)
- Push passive tree toward Lord of Horrors
- Add more Specters as Spirit increases

**Key Milestones:**
- **Act 6+:** Should have 4-6 Specters active
- **Act 10:** Prepare for maps, ensure resistances capped

**Passive Tree Goals:**
- Complete basic minion triangle (Crystine Flesh, Restless Dead, Growing Swarm)
- Start working toward Gravedigger, Necrotized Flesh
- Rush Lord of Horrors

**Gear Upgrades:**
- +1 or +2 to Minion Skills on Helm/Amulet
- 50-100+ Spirit on body armor
- Movement speed boots
- Resistance cap (75%)

#### Maps: Vulgard → Powered Zealots Transition

**Early Maps (White/Yellow):**
- Continue with Vulgard Specters
- Farm currency for gear upgrades
- Focus on Spirit-stacked gear
- Target 300-400 Spirit from gear + 120 from Sacred Flow = 420-520 total

**Mid Maps (Yellow/Red):**
- Monitor your attack speed
- When attack speed is high, Vulgard's 2-second grenade cooldown becomes inefficient
- **Prepare for Powered Zealots transition**

**Powered Zealots Upgrade:**

1. **Complete Third Interlude first:**
   - Get 40 bonus Spirit from Lythara the Wayward Spear (Kriar Village)
2. **Go to Etched Ravine zone:**
   - Find Powered Zealots (lightning beam Specters)
   - Must be **55 Spirit cost**
   - Raise 6-8 Powered Zealots (depending on Spirit)
3. **Update gem setup:**
   - Replace Heft Support with Rapid Attacks
   - Replace Magnified Area with Fire Enchantment
   - Keep Multi-shot, Muster, Feeding Frenzy

**Final Setup:**
```
6L: Raise Spectre (Powered Zealots) + Multi-shot + Muster + Feeding Frenzy + Rapid Attacks + Fire Enchantment
Support: Pain Offering
Curse: Vulnerability
```

**Endgame Goals:**
- 6-8 Powered Zealots active
- 5000+ life, 75% all resistances
- Lord of Horrors completed
- Full defensive layers (ES + Armor)

## FAQ & Resources

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is this good for league start?**
A: Có. Trong PoE2 patch 0.4, đây là league starter rất dễ vào: không cần unique, gần như không cần vốn, và theo GhazzyTV thì leveling từ Act 3 trở đi rất nhẹ. Gameplay cũng đủ đơn giản cho người mới.

**Q: What if I die and lose my Specters?**
A: Return to Utzaal (for Vulgard) or Etched Ravine (for Powered Zealots) and recapture them using Desecrate/Unearth + Raise Spectre. Make sure to get the correct Spirit cost (50 for Vulgard, 55 for Powered Zealots).

**Q: Should I use Charms?**
A: **NO.** Sacred Flow provides 40 Spirit per EMPTY Charm slot. Using Charms would drastically reduce your Spirit pool and cripple the build. Keep all 3 Charm slots empty.

**Q: When should I switch from Vulgard to Powered Zealots?**
A: When your attack speed is high enough that Vulgard's 2-second grenade cooldown feels wasteful. This typically happens in mid-tier maps (yellow/red). Powered Zealots have no cooldown and scale better with attack speed.

**Q: Can this do all content?**
A: Yes. The build scales excellently into endgame with proper Spirit stacking and gear investment. Strong clear speed, solid single-target, and good survivability with minions tanking damage.

**Q: What happened to Wolf minions?**
A: GhazzyTV originally tried Wolf Pack Minion build, but it "failed miserably" with damage described as "absolutely dog shit." Wolf minions have inconsistent tagging (Pounce = "Minion", Wolfpack = "Companion") and deal negligible damage. Specters are vastly superior.

**Q: How much Spirit should I aim for?**
A:
- Act 3: 200-250 (120 from Sacred Flow + 80-130 from gear)
- Early Maps: 300-350 (4-6 Specters)
- Endgame: 400-500+ (6-8 Specters + supports)

**Q: Is this HC viable?**
A: Yes, very HC-friendly. Minions body-block and tank damage, passive playstyle minimizes mechanical mistakes, and strong defensive scaling with ES + Armor.
